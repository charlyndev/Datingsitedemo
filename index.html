Who We Are

Two cultures,
one community

HanoPino is a private, curated community for Filipino–Chinese individuals who value genuine connection, shared roots, and the warmth of belonging. We are not a dating app — we are a home for those who cherish both their Filipino spirit and Chinese heritage.

Whether you seek lifelong friendships, professional networks, or a partner who truly understands your world, our community creates the space for it — with dignity and intention.
